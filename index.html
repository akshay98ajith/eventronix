ABOUT US

Eventronix is a full service event planning and organizing company. We started with a purpose to provide one stop solution for all your event needs. With a focus on long term strategy and growth for our clients, we work with organizations to implement deep-rooted and successful event strategies that address your pain-points and help your organization reach goals.
